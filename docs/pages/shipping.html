Shipping & Delivery — how Kora moves goods to Rwanda

Kora consolidates goods from China, Dubai and South Korea, then routes them via the Dar es Salaam corridor to Rwanda, combining reliability with transparent timelines.

Air corridor

Dubai → Kigali

Typical delivery: 4–10 working days.

Sea corridor

China/Korea → Dar es Salaam → Kigali

Typical delivery: 25–45 days.

Risk & insurance

Risk transfers at Delivery Point

Optional cargo insurance for higher‑value items.

1. From Request to Dispatch

Once you approve TLC and make payment, we coordinate supplier confirmation, QC and consolidation at the relevant hub. We then prepare export documents and book space on the appropriate route (sea or air).

2. Delivery Timeframes

  • Sea shipments: approximately 25–45 days, depending on sailing schedules and customs.
  • Air shipments: approximately 4–10 working days from flight departure.
  • Special cargo or complex consolidations may require additional handling time.

These are estimates, not guaranteed dates; they may change due to inspections, port congestion or other operational factors.

3. Tracking Your Shipment

Kora provides milestone‑based tracking, for example:

  1. Supplier confirmed
  2. Goods received at hub & QC completed
  3. Consolidated for export
  4. Departed origin port/airport
  5. Arrived Dar es Salaam / Kigali
  6. Customs clearance in Rwanda
  7. Out for delivery / Delivered

4. Risk Transfer & Insurance

Unless otherwise agreed, risk transfers to you at the Designated Delivery Point (for example, upon hand‑over to you or your representative in Kigali). You can request optional cargo insurance; terms and coverage depend on the insurer’s policy and the category of goods.
