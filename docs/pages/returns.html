Returns & Refunds — how issues are handled

Because Kora operates a request‑based import model, returns are limited. This page explains when returns are possible, what conditions apply and how we process refunds.

Scope

Request‑based imports

Custom‑requested items have stricter return rules.

Window

Report within 48 hours

For visible damage or mismatches at delivery.

Outcome

Repair, replacement or refund

Depending on supplier/OEM policies.

1. When Returns Are Accepted

Returns may be accepted when:

  • The item delivered is not the product specified in the approved TLC quote.
  • There is a confirmed manufacturing defect not caused by misuse or installation error.
  • Goods are lost or severely damaged in transit and claims are accepted by carriers or insurers.

2. Conditions & Time Windows

  • Items must be unused and returned with original packaging, labels and accessories.
  • Visible damage or mismatch must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Some returns may be subject to re‑export and customs rules, which can affect timing and costs.

3. How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our support team with your order number and a clear description of the issue.
  2. Attach high‑quality photos/videos showing the item, packaging and any damage or defect.
  3. Our team reviews the claim and coordinates with suppliers, carriers and insurers as needed.

We will keep you informed throughout the review process and communicate the final decision as soon as it is available.

4. Non‑Returnable Items

Some categories are generally not eligible for return:

  • Custom electronics and made‑to‑order components.
  • Software‑activated parts and ECUs once activated.
  • Items without export‑return authorization or with hygiene/safety restrictions.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are typically processed within 15–30 days, depending on supplier, carrier and banking workflows. Refunds are credited to the original payment method where possible.

In some cases we may offer credits for future orders instead of cash refunds, particularly for special‑order components where re‑export is not possible.
