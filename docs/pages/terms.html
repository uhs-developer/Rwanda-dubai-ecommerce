2. Nature of Service

Kora acts solely as a procurement and logistics agent. We do not manufacture or resell goods. We work with vetted suppliers in China, Dubai and South Korea based on the specifications you supply, and coordinate QC, consolidation, shipping and customs clearance.

  • You remain the ultimate buyer of the goods.
  • OEM warranties remain between you and the manufacturer; Kora facilitates claims where possible.
  • We may appear as consignee or importer of record in certain documents for customs/logistics efficiency, without changing the underlying relationship.

3. TLC, Quotes & Payment

Before we purchase anything, you receive a TLC quote that includes:

  • Product price and agent commission.
  • FX conversion and international freight/insurance as applicable.
  • Estimated duties, levies, VAT and customs/clearance fees per EACCMA/EAC CET.

Quotes may change before your approval if suppliers re‑quote, FX moves significantly or routes change. Material changes are always re‑confirmed with you.

  • Standard terms: full pre‑payment unless a written credit agreement exists.
  • Special orders or customized items may be non‑cancellable and non‑refundable.
  • Invoices and receipts are issued electronically.
