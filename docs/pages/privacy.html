Core purposes

Procurement, logistics, compliance

To process your requests, move goods and meet customs / AML duties.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to deliver the service and comply with law:

  • Identity & contact: name, phone, email, company, TIN.
  • Order & logistics data: product specs, quotes, tracking, addresses.
  • Payment confirmations: references and status (we do not store full card details).
  • Account & support data: login events, tickets and conversation history with Manzi.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to process requests, issue TLC quotes and deliver orders.
  • Legal obligation: customs, AML/KYC and tax record‑keeping.
  • Legitimate interests: fraud prevention, service improvement and analytics.
  • Consent: where required for marketing or certain cookies (see Cookie Policy).

3. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your data. We share it only with partners necessary to deliver the service:

  • Verified suppliers and sourcing agents in China, Dubai and Korea.
  • Freight forwarders, carriers and last‑mile delivery partners.
  • Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA) and other authorities for customs & compliance.
  • Payment providers and banks to process payments and refunds.
  • Analytics and security tools (with minimized data and appropriate safeguards).

4. Security & Retention

We protect your data using encryption, access controls, secure APIs and regular reviews. Access is limited to staff and partners who need it to perform their role.

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service, handle disputes, and meet legal requirements. When no longer needed, data is anonymized or deleted.

5. Your Rights

Under Rwanda data protection law you may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion where legally possible.
  • Request restriction or object to certain processing.
  • Request data portability where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for non‑essential processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact our DPO at [ADD_DPO_EMAIL].

6. Cross‑Border Transfers

Because we source from China, Dubai and South Korea, your data may be processed outside Rwanda. We use contractual and technical safeguards and share only what is strictly necessary for each transaction.
