Dispute Resolution — how Kora handles complaints and conflicts

We aim to resolve issues quickly and fairly. This page explains our escalation path, from support ticket to amicable resolution and, if needed, KIAC arbitration.

1. Step One — Talk to Us

If you have a concern about a quote, shipment, return or invoice, please contact our support team first. Most issues can be resolved quickly through clarification or corrective action.

Support email: support@example.com

2. Amicable Resolution Window

Both parties agree to attempt an amicable resolution for at least 30 days from the date one party notifies the other of a dispute. During this period, we will:

  • Review the facts and documentation provided.
  • Engage with suppliers and logistics partners where relevant.
  • Propose fair remedies where the issue stems from our processes or partners.

3. Arbitration at KIAC

If a dispute cannot be resolved amicably within 30 days, either party may refer the dispute to binding arbitration under the Kigali International Arbitration Centre (KIAC) Rules.

  • Seat of arbitration: Kigali, Rwanda.
  • Language: English.
  • Tribunal: one arbitrator, unless the parties agree otherwise.
  • Award: final and enforceable in accordance with Rwanda law.
