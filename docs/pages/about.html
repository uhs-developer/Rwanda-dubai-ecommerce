Mission & Vision

Mission: Remove inventory risk for Rwandan innovators and businesses by offering request‑based global procurement with transparent Total Landed Cost (TLC), rigorous supplier verification and reliable end‑to‑end logistics.

Vision: A Rwanda where access to specialized parts and components is as easy as clicking “request” — enabling faster prototyping, affordable repairs and thriving local manufacturing.

Our Story

2023 — Problem spotted

Local innovators and garages were paying extreme premiums for low‑turn parts. We saw the opportunity to cut the premium by removing inventory risk.

2024 — Kora is founded

Built around the TLC model and three sourcing hubs: China, Dubai and South Korea.

Today

We operate consolidation hubs, trusted agent networks and a transparent procurement engine used by innovators, garages and SMEs.

How Kora Works — Simple

  1. Request: You submit the product spec — make, model, or part number.
  2. Quote (TLC): We return a fully itemized Total Landed Cost — product, FX, freight, duties and fees.
  3. Approve & Pay: You approve TLC and pay; we source the product and perform QC at origin.
  4. Ship & Track: Consolidated shipping (sea through Dar or air via Dubai) with milestone tracking.
  5. Deliver: Customs clearance and last‑mile delivery to your door in Rwanda.

Frequently Asked (short)

Do you hold stock?

No. Everything is procured on request to avoid inventory risk and high local premiums.

How is price calculated?

We provide a full TLC: product cost + FX + freight + duties + handling + service fees.

Which hubs do you use?

China for electronics, Dubai for air consolidation & re‑export, and Korea for specialized auto parts.

How long does shipping take?

Sea shipments: ~25–45 days. Air shipments: ~4–10 working days depending on customs & consolidation.
