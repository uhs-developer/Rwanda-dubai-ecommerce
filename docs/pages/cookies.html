1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about you — like your session, preferences or what is in your cart.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: required for login, cart and security. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: help us measure traffic, search performance and errors.
  • Preference cookies: remember your language, currency and view settings.

3. Managing Your Preferences

When you first visit Kora you will see a cookie banner. You can accept all cookies or customize your choices. You can change these choices at any time using the “Cookie Settings” link in the footer.

You may also control cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of Kora may not function correctly if you block essential cookies.

4. Third‑Party Cookies

Some analytics or performance tools may set their own cookies. We only work with providers that respect your privacy and are contractually bound to process data on our behalf.
